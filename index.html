I am Samia Islam. I am a PhD student in the Bhattacharya Lab at Michigan State University. My advisor is Dr. Sudin Bhattacharya. My research field is focused on Dynamical Systems in Single-Cell Genomics. Currently I am trying to come up with an algorithm to derive single-cell dynamical models.

Education

  • PhD (Ongoing), Department of Computer Science, Michigan State University
  • BSc, Department of Computer Science, Islamic University of Technology (IUT), Bangladesh

Research Interests

  • Dynamical Systems
  • Non-Linear Dynamics
  • Single-Cell Analysis
  • Deep Learning
