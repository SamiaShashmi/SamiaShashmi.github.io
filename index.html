I am Samia Islam. I am a PhD student in the Bhattacharya Lab at Michigan State University under the supervision of Dr. Sudin Bhattacharya and Dr. Zijun Cui. My research field is focused on Dynamical Systems in Single-Cell Genomics. My aim is to develop an algorithm to derive single-cell dynamical models.
